Schools

We empower schools with the tools they need to maximize the productivity of their Teachers and analyze the performance of their students. An intuitive, accessible interface allows Schools to migrate their entire homework system online with a few easy clicks.

Students

We provide students with an easy to navigate interface to solve HW problems assigned to them in class. Each problem has a associated equation sheet for reference and also has a special calculator integrated within it. Our module focuses on classes 8-10 in the CBSE board.

Teachers

Teachers can create virtual class rooms and assign homework from our extensive database or use our templates to create their own questions. HW is automatically graded so the teachers can focus less on correction and more on education. After every assignment, teachers are provided with a short analysis of their class result so that they have a clear idea of how much their students are grasping in class.

Parents

We believe that it is important and helpful for the parents to be actively involved in their child's education. Parents can create an account and follow their child's progress without being intrusive or disruptive. We value our student's privacy though and it is up to the student to choose how much to share.
