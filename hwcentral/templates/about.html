About Us

HWCentral is an Internet platform built specifically to improve how students learn outside of the classroom. It streamlines how home work is assigned and corrected, freeing up teachers and schools to focus on what they do best - teaching. Targeted questions, an expansive question bank and an immersive interface allow schools to reduce repetitive correction and concentrate on educating and evaluating their students. The platform also uses state of the art analytics to provide teachers and parents with a detailed understanding of student weaknesses and how they can be corrected.

Education just entered the 21st century.
