HomeWork Central was founded in the summer of 2013 by a group of young entrepreneurs committed to create an online tool for homework and learning in India. It is currently under development and scheduled for a beta launch in January 2014.

Meet Our Team

Sharang Pai

Sharang currently studies in the 11th grade and is very interested in the applications of online games as educational tools. He founded HomeWork Central to create an accessible, high quality tool that could supplement primary and secondary education in India.

Oasis Vali

Oasis is a fourth year student at the University of Waterloo studying 'Mechatronics'. He is passionate about integrating technology with the educational system and, having grown up in India, is familiar with the system. He also has a lot of previous experience working on back-end web applications.

Sidhant Pai

Sidhant is a fourth year student at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and is very interested in integrating informal teaching techniques with mainstream education in India. Born and raised in India, he is very familiar with the system and also has previous experience in teaching roles.
